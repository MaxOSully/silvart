Artist statement

My work embraces small shifts in color, repetition, and the quiet imperfections of hand-drawn marks. Each piece is built in layers, revealing traces of decisions left visible rather than erased.

I’m interested in how memory fragments, how places blur over time, and how texture can hold a story without ever fully explaining it.
